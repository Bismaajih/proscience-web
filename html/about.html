About PROSCIENCE

Empowering professionals through innovative training and education

Our Mission

PROSCIENCE Training Center was founded with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical application in scientific and technological fields. We believe that continuous learning and skill development are essential for professional growth and organizational success.

Since our establishment in 2010, we have been committed to providing high-quality training programs that combine theoretical foundations with hands-on practical experience. Our approach ensures that participants can immediately apply what they learn to their professional roles.

We serve professionals across various industries, including healthcare, engineering, information technology, and environmental sciences, helping them stay at the forefront of their fields.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading provider of professional training programs in Southeast Asia, recognized for our excellence in scientific and technological education. We aim to create a community of lifelong learners who drive innovation and progress in their respective fields.

By 2030, we envision expanding our reach to serve professionals across Asia, developing new programs in emerging technologies, and establishing strategic partnerships with leading academic institutions and industry organizations.

We measure our success not just by the number of participants we train, but by the tangible impact our programs have on their careers and the organizations they serve.

Our History

Key milestones in our journey

2010

Foundation

PROSCIENCE was founded by Dr. Sarah Johnson with a small team of 5 professionals, offering our first certification program in Laboratory Techniques.

2013

First International Program

We expanded our reach beyond Indonesia, conducting our first international training program in Singapore.

2015

New Training Facility

Opened our state-of-the-art training center in Jakarta, equipped with modern laboratories and technology-enhanced classrooms.

2018

10,000th Participant

Celebrated training our 10,000th participant across all programs, a testament to our growing impact.

2020

Digital Transformation

Launched our online learning platform in response to the pandemic, allowing us to continue serving professionals worldwide.

2023

Regional Expansion

Established partnerships with institutions in Malaysia, Thailand, and Vietnam to deliver localized training programs.
