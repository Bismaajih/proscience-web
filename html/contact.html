Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our hotline number.

What payment methods do you accept for training programs?

We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and several digital payment methods including OVO, GoPay, and Dana.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the appropriate team member is available to assist you.

Do you offer customized training programs for organizations?

Yes, we specialize in developing tailored training solutions for corporate and institutional clients. Contact our partnership team to discuss your specific needs.
